AAR Music Mix

Formed in 1999, The All-American Rejects were one of the first groups to take emo into the mainstream, although the Oklahoma natives quickly evolved beyond the scene's narrow confines. By pairing anthemic alt-rock with punk and power pop, the band scored a stream of hits throughout the ‘00s and sold over 10 million albums worldwide. Whether you prefer their stripped-down, emotionally raw material or the anthems that sparked sing-alongs in basement parties from coast to coast, this mix has it alls The Mix

Rocksound.tv interview with Tyson

The All-American Rejects frontman Tyson Ritter chats with us at When We Were Young Festival 2022 . He talks us through their tiny pop pup show at SoulBelly BBQ in Las Vegas the night before, put together after the festival’s first day was cancelled due to weather issues. Plus, we find out about his new band, Now More Than Ever, who just released their first single ‘Don’t Rush, Don’t Wait’. We also look back on 20 years since the release of Rejects’ self-titled album which featured the classic ‘Swing, Swing’.
